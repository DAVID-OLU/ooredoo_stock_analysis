# ooredoo_stock_analysis
Ooredoo Stock Analysis Dashboard: Price Trends, Performance &amp; Market Insights

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze Ooredoo’s stock performance over time and uncover key insights about price trends, trading volume, volatility, and overall market behavior using interactive dashboards.

## 📁 Dataset

Download Dashboard and files here:
(https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/187RzrT5HnjEiZeFqDeGS_8ul77UdJmNg?usp=drive_link)

## ⚙️ Methodology

### Data Preparation

Cleaned historical stock price data

Handled missing values and inconsistent trading days

Converted date field into structured time format (Year, Quarter, Month)

Standardized numerical fields (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume)

Data Modeling
Structured the dataset for analysis by:

Time (Daily / Monthly / Quarterly)

Price movement (Open, Close, High, Low)

Volume traded

Returns (Daily/Period performance)

## 📊 Visualization (Power BI)

📌 Page 1: Overview Dashboard

Volume trend visualization

📌 Page 2: Price Analysis

Volatility trends

## 📈 Key Insights

-> Overall Price Movement
Ooredoo stock shows periods of stability with occasional spikes driven by market activity and earnings announcements.

-> Volatility Trends
Volatility tends to increase during specific market cycles, indicating reaction to financial results or broader economic conditions.

-> Trading Volume Behavior
Higher trading volumes often align with major price movements, suggesting strong investor response during key events.

-> Long-Term Trend
The stock generally shows a stable long-term movement with moderate growth/decline depending on the selected time range.


## 💡 Business Implications
Helps investors identify entry and exit timing based on price trends

Supports risk assessment through volatility analysis

Volume spikes can signal institutional interest or market news impact
